About

Print & Digital Design, Brand Communication, Visual Identity Systems, Illustration

This is a selection of works by Sarah Beth Martin. Currently working in Charlottesville, Virginia. Fascinated by color, typography, composition, patterns and movement. Whether in the digital realm or on a three-dimensional scale, I combine different disciplines and mediums to build engaging experiences.

I'm a little obsessed with my three fluffy felines and three not-so-fluffy tortoises. You may also find me painting with watercolors, sewing with felt, gaming, on the tennis court or at the bowling lanes.
