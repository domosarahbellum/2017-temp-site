Mosaic Identity

Visual Identity System

Client: MicroAire Surgical Instruments, LLC. Biologics Division

MicroAire aquired a PRP (Platelet Rich Plasma) System, a system designed to extract platelet rich plasma from blood.

Goals: The PRP System needed to stand out and express the story of a quality device that is superior to the competition in an aggressive and overcrowded market. The brand's identity should be simple, must work as a system, flexible, and be easily identifiable in the field.

Team: Sarah Beth Martin: Designer ● Elias Jones: Creative Services Lead

Logo and Typeface

This particular system needed to stand on its own, apart from the parent company. While its visuals should tie back to the company, they shouldn't be derivitive of the company's logo and color scheme (like previous products) as they would not help differentiate the system from the competition.

For the logo we used a simplistic, grotesque typeface, Swiss 721, as a base. This particular typeface had a history of usage throughout much of the company's collateral.

The logo also required the ability to add devices to the Mosaic System. At the time those products included PRP (Platelet Rich Plasma), AS (Autologous Serum a.k.a. PPP or Platelet Poor Plasma), and phlebotomy (equipment to draw blood). The logo was created to be flexible enough to accomodate additional devices in the future.

Color Scheme

Target surgeons are primarily in orthopaedics, but visuals should be flexible to work in the vastly different aesthetic market in the future.

As the name implys, the system of devices should work together. Similar to a mosaic, each "piece" (device) can be put together to create an image (system).

We use complimentary colors to easiliy identify each product within the system. The blue is the main choice for the system's primary product, the PRP device. The primary benefit of the PRP device is how it is cleaner (with less red blood cells) than the competition and blue best represented that cleanliness. The red represents the blood cells in PPP (for AS). The additional colors, green and orange, are complimentary of those colors for the future expansion of additional devices in the system.

Packaging

It's imperitive for the packaging of the Mosaic System and its individual devices to be easily recognizable, particularly on the storage shelves of hospitals/practices.

For easier identification by the users, each package is color-coded depending on the device (or combination of devices) within on the front and the sides.
