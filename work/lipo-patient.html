Patient Website

Web Design and Development

Client: MicroAire Surgical Instruments, LLC

Goals: Redesign the online experience to improve navigation on desktop and mobile devices. Improve search engine optimization and create a memorable experience for users interested in learning about power-assisted liposuction.

Team: Sarah Beth Martin: Design & Development ● Elias Jones: Creative Services Lead

Website Redesign

As an ongoing initiative to increase patient knowledge on product-specific procedures, MicroAire needed to update their patient materials including an informative pamphlet and website on the power-assisted liposuction procedure. In response to MicroAire's corporate refresh of the brand, these materials also needed to resemble the updated brand.

The team recognized that the previous website was missing a few fundamental functionality features that are essential to increasing patient interest as well as organic traffic such as simplified and useful content, a responsive framework (as mobile access to online information is increasing), and a useful feature for patients to request surgeons in their area.

The static site included obsolete features no longer supported by today's browsers which also may have contributed to a lack of organic traffic. The patient website also had not been updated since it was released several years prior and incorporated old branding elements which presented seemingly dated information.
